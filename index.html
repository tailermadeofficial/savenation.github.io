About SaveMyNation

Save My Nation is a student community open source initiative to mitigate the impact of COVID-19 using technology as a tool. We are a group of 5000+ volunteers from various parts of India who primarily focus on major problems like migration, supply of material to affected victims. We are looking for more volunteers from various technological, social and call centre domains. We already have a help line consisting of 25+ volunteers to address problems that the government faces.
